What Takt does

Everything you need to keep your browser under control.

Rule-Based Grouping

Create rules that match tabs by URL or title. Use simple text matching, domain matching, glob patterns, or full regex.

Duplicate Prevention Premium

Open the same URL twice? Takt catches it, closes the duplicate, and focuses the original.

Resource Intelligence Premium

See which tab groups consume the most memory. Get health scores, idle tracking, and discard recommendations.

Hibernation Free & Premium

Fully close idle tabs to reclaim memory, then restore them instantly with all state preserved. Manual is free; auto-triggers are Premium.

Sessions Premium

Snapshot all your tab groups into a named session. Restore later in a new window or replace the current one.

Sync Across Devices Premium

Sync rules and profiles via a local folder (Google Drive, Dropbox, iCloud) or directly through Google Drive.
